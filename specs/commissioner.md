Every league is ran by a commissioner or group of commissioners that serve as the administrators. They are responsible for gathering players for the league, making sure there are the right amount of teams, and coordinating logistics with the golf course the league plays at.

While a lot of that is outside of the scope of our mobile app, they will need to occassionaly do admin work in Greenie.

## Accounts

Everyone that uses Greenie needs to make an account in order to join leagues. This would be a basic account with no special permissions. A commissioner, on the other hand, will have additional permissions that allow them access to special functionality.

We will need someone way to upgrade standard users to admins, based on league.

## Abilities

The main abilites an admin needs is the ability to send messages to the league (maybe we could eventually have a league chat feature), optionally with push notifications. Finally, they need to be able to edit scorecards to ensure correctness (players will be recording score on a physical scorecard and they will need to be uploaded somehow).

Ideally, we can automate this process or have players edit their own scores in the app, but occasionally the commissioner will have to do this or make corrections (corrections is the most common need).

In addition, admins will set the schedule, including starting times for each team/week/round and what hole the league starts on (it will either be the front nine or back nine), as well as set up and distribute proxies and skins (which can be a later feature)
